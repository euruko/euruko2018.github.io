Accessibility information

The facts:

  • The venue is easily accessible by public transportation(we need to check this!)
  • The hotel and the restrooms are wheelchair-accessible. Sadly there's no elevator to the balustrade of the ballroom
  • We're working to get a stenographer in order to offer a live translation but we cannot guarantee that at this point

We're taking the well-being of every attendee really serious. Please let us know on twitter or via email if we can help you in any way.
